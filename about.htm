About IWB Tech

IWB is a technology company based in Maseru, Lesotho that specializes in computer component recycling and software solutions. We are committed to reducing electronic waste by repurposing and recycling parts such as hard drives, motherboard components, and RAM modules. In addition to hardware recycling, we also offer cutting-edge software tools for data recovery and destruction, ensuring that businesses and individuals can manage their digital assets securely.
